Hi! My name is Mahdi Najafi-Ziyazi.

I'm a PhD student at the Centre for mathematical Plasma Astrophysics (CmPA), KU Leuven. I'm part of an international research team led by Prof. Dr. Stefaan Poedts, dedicated to developing realistic computational models of the solar atmosphere. My research specifically targets the lower solar atmosphere, where I am working to create a multi-fluid model of the solar chromosphere.
